Welcome to Srilankan Food recipes

Sri Lanka is a country which have different nationalities and therefore there are different food cultures. Srilankans' main meal is rice and curry. Some people prefers rice for all three meals of the day while some of them use different short and sweet meals like bread,hoppers,string hoppers,milkrice. So the aim of this website is to provide you some recipes of different short meals which you can prepare in your home kitchen.
