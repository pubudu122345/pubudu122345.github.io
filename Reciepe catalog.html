Lets look at some popular breakfast meals people love to prepare at home. You can click on the name of the food to find more information and the recipe of that food.

1. Hoppers

Hoppers are a popular dish in South India and Sri Lanka that are thin pancakes in the shape of bowls made from fermented rice (or rice flour) and coconut milk. 

 

2. String hoppers

Rice flour (or finger millet flour) is squeezed into a press to create thin noodles for string hoppers, a traditional Sri Lankan and South Indian specialty that is steamed and served with coconut sambol or coconut milk gravy. 

 

 

3. Milk rice

A traditional cake-shaped dish from Sri Lanka, milk rice is made with rice and coconut milk. It is a customary breakfast in Sri Lanka as well as a celebratory treat for all special occasions.

 

4. Ulundu wade

Spicy lentil fritters known as "ulundu wade" are highly favored in southern India and Sri Lanka. They are frequently eaten with sambar for breakfast or as snacks.
